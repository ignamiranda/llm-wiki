# Riad Djemili

## Bio

Riad Djemili is the creator of Curious Expedition at Maschinen-Mensch. He developed a three-layer ruleset for procedural storytelling — a world layer, an event layer, and a sentence layer — that creates personal storylines without a dedicated story system. He authored Chapter 11 "Memorable Stories from Simple Rules in Curious Expedition."

## Key Contributions

- Created Curious Expedition at Maschinen-Mensch
- Developed the three-layer ruleset for procedural storytelling (world, event, sentence)
- Demonstrated how simple rules can generate memorable personal storylines
- Authored Chapter 11 "Memorable Stories from Simple Rules in Curious Expedition"
