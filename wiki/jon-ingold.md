# Jon Ingold

## Bio

Jon Ingold is the co-founder of Inkle, the studio behind 80 Days, Sorcery!, and Heaven's Vault. He created the Ink narrative scripting language, which pioneered the Weave Structure (Ink's core flow construct that organizes knots, stitches, and diverts), and developed the concepts of narrative momentum and Only Forwards Design. His work focuses on designing interactive narratives that feel authored yet responsive. He authored Chapter 7 "Designing for Narrative Momentum" in Procedural Storytelling in Game Design.

## Key Contributions

- Created the Ink narrative scripting language and Weave Structure
- Developed the concept of narrative momentum for interactive fiction
- Pioneered Only Forwards Design: narrative that always pushes forward
