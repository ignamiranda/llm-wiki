# Matthew Bozarth

## Bio

Matthew Bozarth is a game developer at Undead Labs who contributed to the procedural character generation system in State of Decay 2, focusing on the character trait system and contradiction winnowing. He co-authored Chapter 22 "Procedural Characters in State of Decay 2."

## Key Contributions

- Contributed to State of Decay 2's procedural character generation at Undead Labs
- Focused on the character trait system and contradiction winnowing
- Co-authored Chapter 22 "Procedural Characters in State of Decay 2"
