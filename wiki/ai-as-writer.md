# AI as Writer

## Definition

The role an AI plays in [[compile-time-knowledge]] systems, particularly the [[wiki-compile-pattern]]. The AI reads raw material, synthesizes understanding, writes wiki pages, connects new links, adds concept explanations, cross-references, creates indexes, and flags contradictions. It makes editorial judgment calls about importance, connections, and framing — effectively doing the work of a research assistant who maintains a living document.

## Key Properties

- Intensive interaction when new information arrives
- Adding a single research paper may trigger updates across a dozen pages
- Heavy front-end operation, cheap subsequent queries
- Requires trust in AI's editorial decisions
- Best for deep research with evolving understanding
