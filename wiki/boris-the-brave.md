# Boris the Brave

## Bio / 简介

Boris the Brave (Boris van Schooten) is a developer known for creating DeBroglie, a comprehensive C# WFC library that supports hex grids, additional constraints, and backtracking. He independently applied Paul Merrell's modifying-in-blocks technique to the lazy generation of unbounded tile configurations, and developed the chiseling method for guaranteeing connected structures with WFC. He also published a commercial Unity asset based on the simple tiled model.

## Key Contributions / 主要贡献

- Created DeBroglie — the most feature-complete C# WFC library
- Developed the [[chiseling-method]] for guaranteed connected output
- Adapted [[modifying-in-blocks]] for lazy infinite WFC generation
- Published a commercial Unity asset based on the simple tiled model
