# University of Alberta Quest Research Group

## Definition / 定义

The quest generation research conducted by Nathan Sturtevant's group at the University of Alberta, applying search-based AI techniques to the problem of generating structured quest content for games. The group's approach is rooted in their broader expertise in pathfinding, planning, and procedural content generation.

## Key Properties / 关键特性

- **Group lead**: [[nathan-sturtevant]]
- **Institution**: University of Alberta, Department of Computing Science
- **Methodological focus**: Search-based procedural content generation, planning, constraint satisfaction
- **Research context**: Extends from the group's core work in pathfinding (HPA*, JPS) and GVG-AI to content generation

## Key Outputs / 主要产出

- [[2020-01-01-yu-quest-generation]] — procedural quest generation paper
- Various GVG-AI framework contributions
