# Geoffrey Card

## Bio

Geoffrey Card is a game designer at Undead Labs who worked on State of Decay 2's procedural character system. He co-developed the contradiction winnowing system — a tag-based approach to generating believable characters by eliminating conflicting trait combinations. He co-authored Chapter 22 "Procedural Characters in State of Decay 2."

## Key Contributions

- Worked on State of Decay 2's procedural character generation system at Undead Labs
- Co-developed the contradiction winnowing system for believable character generation
- Co-authored Chapter 22 "Procedural Characters in State of Decay 2"
