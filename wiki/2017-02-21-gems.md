# February Update: Gems

## Summary

Collectible gems were added to Eye of the Temple, placed at comfortable reach distances tuned for varying player heights, accompanied by analysis of critical versus non-critical progression rewards.

## Content

The February 2017 update introduced collectible gems as a reward system in Eye of the Temple. A key design concern was placement: gems needed to be within comfortable reach distance for players of varying heights, since VR interactions rely on the player's real physical reach.

The post also explored the distinction between critical and non-critical progression rewards. Critical items (keys, switches) gate progress and must be intuitive to find, while non-critical items (gems) provide optional incentives for exploration without blocking advancement. This distinction helped shape the pacing and reward structure of the temple.

## Key Takeaways

- Gems placed at comfortable reach for varying player heights
- Analysis of critical vs non-critical progression rewards
- Optional collectibles incentivize exploration without blocking progress
