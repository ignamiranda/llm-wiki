# The Cluster is Now Released

## Summary

The Cluster was released for free on Itch.io after 21 years of on-and-off development. It is a 2.5D procedurally generated exploration platformer that uses the LayerProcGen framework and a planning-based approach to create infinite worlds.

## Content

First prototyped in 2003, The Cluster's development properly began in 2008 and continued through 2016, when it was put on hold due to burdensome design constraints detailed in a postmortem. Eight years later, the game was polished and finally released in 2024 as a free title.

The game uses the LayerProcGen framework for procedural world generation, enabling infinite exploration in a 2.5D space. A lightweight planning approach ensures that the generated worlds are coherent and navigable. The release marked the culmination of the longest-running project in its creator's career, spanning more than two decades from first concept to public release.
