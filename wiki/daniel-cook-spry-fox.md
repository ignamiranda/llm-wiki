# Daniel Cook

## Bio

Daniel Cook is the co-founder of Spry Fox, the studio behind Triple Town and Road Not Taken. He developed an emotion engineering framework — adapting Bura's work — for amplifying themes and emotions through game systems. His work on Triple Town is a case study in both successful theme amplification and failures of communication. He authored Chapter 12 "Amplifying Themes and Emotions in Systems."

## Key Contributions

- Co-founded Spry Fox, the studio behind Triple Town and Road Not Taken
- Developed an emotion engineering framework for amplifying themes through game systems
- Analyzed Triple Town as a case study in theme amplification and communication failures
- Authored Chapter 12 "Amplifying Themes and Emotions in Systems"
