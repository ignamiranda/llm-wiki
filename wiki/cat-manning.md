# Cat Manning

## Bio

Cat Manning is a writer and game designer who analyzed tarot as an analogue procedural storytelling system, identifying how its overlapping taxonomies — suits, numbers, Major and Minor Arcana, spread positions, upright and reversed — avoid the oatmeal problem. She authored Chapter 26 "Tarot as Procedural Storytelling."
