# Marta Fijak

## Bio

Marta Fijak is a narrative designer at 11 Bit Studios (Warsaw, Poland). She co-authored Chapter 19 ("Beyond Fun in Frostpunk") in the 2019 edited collection *Procedural Storytelling in Game Design*, exploring how Frostpunk's mechanics drive narrative meaning beyond traditional entertainment boundaries.

## Key Contributions

- Narrative design on Frostpunk (11 Bit Studios)
- Analysis of systemic storytelling in games with moral weight
