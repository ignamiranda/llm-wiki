# Ian Parberry

## Bio / 简介

Ian Parberry is a professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at the University of North Texas, where he directs the Laboratory for Recreational Computing (LARC). His research spans procedural content generation, game development, and algorithm analysis. He has published extensively on procedural quest generation, including the influential structural analysis of MMORPG quests.

## Key Contributions / 主要贡献

- Co-author of "A Prototype Quest Generator Based on a Structural Analysis of Quests from Four MMORPGs" (2011) with Jonathan Doran
- Director of the Laboratory for Recreational Computing (LARC) at UNT
- Published multiple technical reports on procedural quest generation (LARC series)
- Research on structural analysis of quests from major MMORPGs
