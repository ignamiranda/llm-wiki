# Jongwoo Kim

## Bio

Jongwoo Kim is a game designer at Kitfox Games who created The Shrouded Isle. He developed a minimal trait-based personality generation system — family plus one virtue and one vice per character — with three states of trait knowledge (unrevealed to partially to fully revealed), creating compelling moral quandaries through character systems. He authored Chapter 24 "Generating Personalities in The Shrouded Isle."

## Key Contributions

- Created The Shrouded Isle at Kitfox Games
- Developed a minimal trait-based personality generation system (family + 1 virtue + 1 vice)
- Designed three-state trait knowledge progression (unrevealed → partially → fully revealed)
- Authored Chapter 24 "Generating Personalities in The Shrouded Isle"
