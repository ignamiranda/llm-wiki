# Dr. Michael Cook

## Bio

Dr. Michael Cook is a researcher at Queen Mary University of London specializing in AI and game design. He developed the foundational framework for ethical procedural generation, identifying how generators encode creators' values and amplify them at scale — meaning that biases and assumptions in generation rules propagate exponentially. His work includes the game "A Rogue Dream" and analysis of the Tay chatbot problem as a cautionary tale for generative systems. He authored Chapter 5 "Ethical Procedural Generation" in Procedural Storytelling in Game Design.

## Key Contributions

- Developed the foundational framework for ethical procedural generation
- Identified how generators encode creator values and amplify them at scale
- Analyzed the Tay chatbot problem as a warning for generative system design
