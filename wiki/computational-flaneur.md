# Computational Flâneur

## Definition

Mx. Lazer-Walker's concept of a computational flâneur is a site-specific generative poetry walk. GPS-triggered neural net or Markov chain text combines with the physical environment to create emergent synchrony between generated content and the real-world location the player walks through.

## Key Properties

- **Site-specific generative poetry** — Text is generated in response to the player's physical location
- **GPS-region transitions** — Moving between GPS-defined regions triggers new text generation
- **Neural net / Markov chain text** — Text is generated using statistical models trained on poetry or prose
- **Emergent synchrony with physical environment** — The most powerful moments occur when generated text coincidentally matches the physical surroundings
- **Variable reward systems for poetry** — The unpredictability of quality creates a variable reward loop, making rare good lines feel special

## Examples

Lazer-Walker describes walking through a city while receiving procedurally generated poetry triggered by location. When the generated text happens to describe something visible in the environment, it creates a powerful moment of perceived meaning.
