# June Update: Verticality, Puzzles, Whip

## Summary

Development progress on Eye of the Temple including a whip prototype for grabbing levers at distance, rotating tower puzzles with two-level design, and increased verticality in VR level layout.

## Content

The June 2017 update for Eye of the Temple introduced several new mechanics and level design directions. A whip prototype was implemented, allowing players to grab levers from a distance and yank them, extending the interactive reach beyond the player's physical arm length.

Rotating tower puzzles were added with a two-level design, requiring players to solve spatial relationships between floors. The level design also pushed toward greater verticality, using the player's real physical space to navigate multi-height environments.

## Key Takeaways

- Whip can grab levers at distance and yank
- Rotating towers with two-level puzzle design
- Increased verticality in VR level layout
