# Dex Hardy

## Bio

Dex Hardy is the founder of Human Layer, a company focused on improving human-AI interaction. He coined the influential "Smart Zone / Dumb Zone" concept, which describes how language models perform optimally in fresh sessions and degrade quadratically in quality as the context window accumulates tokens due to attention relationship scaling.

## Key Contributions

- **Smart Zone / Dumb Zone** — Identified and named the phenomenon of LLM quality degradation with context accumulation
- **Human Layer** — Founded company improving human-AI collaboration
