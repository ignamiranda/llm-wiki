# Matthew Keeter

## Bio

Matthew Keeter is a software engineer and researcher known for creating libfive, a comprehensive infrastructure for functional representation (f-rep) solid modeling. His work focuses on the intersection of computer graphics, computational geometry, and functional programming, producing tools that treat 3D modeling as a mathematical and programmatic discipline.

## Key Contributions

- Creator of libfive — a full f-rep solid modeling system with a C++ shared library, Guile Scheme and Python bindings, and a Studio GUI
- Advances in functional representation meshing — producing watertight, manifold, feature-preserving triangle meshes from mathematical shape definitions
- Open-source contributions to the procedural modeling and CAD ecosystem
