# Tim Keenan

## Bio

Tim Keenan is the co-creator of Duskers, a game about controlling drones to explore derelict spaceships. He developed techniques for curated narrative where static, hand-crafted content is fragmented and reordered to produce different meanings in procedural contexts. He co-authored Chapter 8 "Curated Narrative in Duskers."

## Key Contributions

- Co-created Duskers, a procedural narrative game about drone exploration
- Developed curated narrative techniques that fragment static content for procedural reordering
- Co-authored Chapter 8 "Curated Narrative in Duskers"
