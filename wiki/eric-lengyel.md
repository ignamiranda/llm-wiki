# Eric Lengyel

## Bio

Eric Lengyel is a computer graphics researcher specializing in real-time rendering, voxel algorithms, and game engine technology. He is the creator of the Transvoxel Algorithm for seamless level-of-detail transitions in voxel terrain, and the editor/author of the "Game Engine Gems" book series.

## Key Contributions

- Creator of the Transvoxel Algorithm
- Author of "Game Engine Gems" series
- Contributor to real-time rendering literature
