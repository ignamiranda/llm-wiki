# Weixiao Liu

## Bio

Weixiao Liu is a researcher at National University of Singapore and Johns Hopkins University, affiliated with the Chirikjian Lab. He is the lead author of Marching-Primitives, a CVPR 2023 Highlight paper that extracts superquadric primitives directly from signed distance functions.

## Key Contributions

- Lead author of Marching-Primitives (CVPR 2023 Highlight)
- Research in shape abstraction, 3D perception, and robotic manipulation
- Developed method for extracting geometric primitives from SDFs
