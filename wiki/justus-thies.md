# Justus Thies

## Bio

Justus Thies is a computer vision researcher at Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems, known for pioneering work in neural rendering (e.g., Deferred Neural Rendering, Neural Textures), real-time face tracking and reenactment, and 3D reconstruction. He created PyMarchingCubes, a fork of PyMCubes with improved triangulation and color interpolation support.

## Key Contributions

- Created PyMarchingCubes — improved marching cubes with color interpolation
- Pioneered neural rendering approaches (Neural Textures, Deferred Neural Rendering)
- Research in real-time face reenactment and reconstruction
