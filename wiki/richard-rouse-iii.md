# Richard Rouse III

## Bio

Richard Rouse III is a game designer and narrative director at Paranoid Productions. He developed the concept of "heavily authored dynamic storytelling" — a middle path between pure procedural generation and fixed linear narrative — demonstrated in Church in the Darkness, a game about exploring a 1970s cult commune. He has also worked on The Suffering and Midway games, bringing narrative depth to action-oriented genres. He authored Chapter 14 "Heavily Authored Dynamic Storytelling in Church in the Darkness" in Procedural Storytelling in Game Design.

## Key Contributions

- Developed "heavily authored dynamic storytelling" as a middle path between procedural and fixed narrative
- Demonstrated the approach in Church in the Darkness
- Advanced understanding of curated narrative in procedurally varied game worlds
