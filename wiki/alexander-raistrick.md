# Alexander Raistrick

## Bio

Alexander Raistrick is a researcher in procedural 3D scene generation, affiliated with Princeton University. He is the lead author of Infinigen Indoors (CVPR 2024) and ProcFunc (2026), and a co-author of Infinigen-Articulated (2025). His work focuses on extending the [[infinigen-procedural-scenes]] ecosystem with new scene types and higher-level abstractions for procedural generation.

## Key Contributions

- **Infinigen Indoors** (CVPR 2024): Extending Infinigen from outdoor to indoor scenes with constraint-based layout
- **ProcFunc** (2026): Function-oriented abstractions for Blender procedural generation, designed for VLM code editing
- **Infinigen-Articulated** (2025, co-author): Procedural generation of articulated assets for robotics
