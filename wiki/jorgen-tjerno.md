# Jørgen Tjernø

## Bio

Jørgen Tjernø is a game developer at Undead Labs who contributed to State of Decay 2's procedural character system. He co-developed the tag-based contradiction winnowing approach and the hierarchical trait tree system. He co-authored Chapter 22 "Procedural Characters in State of Decay 2."

## Key Contributions

- Contributed to State of Decay 2's procedural character system at Undead Labs
- Co-developed the contradiction winnowing approach for trait combination filtering
- Co-developed the hierarchical trait tree system
- Co-authored Chapter 22 "Procedural Characters in State of Decay 2"
