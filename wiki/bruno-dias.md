# Bruno Dias

## Bio

Bruno Dias is a game developer who created Voyageur, a game of procedural exploration and description. He developed the Improv grammar library, a Tracery-like system with world model awareness, and the filter stack architecture for procedural text generation. His filter stack addresses both the "10,000 Bowls of Oatmeal Problem" (perceptual uniqueness) and the "Brutalist Building Problem" (coherence and consistency) simultaneously. He authored Chapter 16 "Procedural Descriptions in Voyageur" in Procedural Storytelling in Game Design.

## Key Contributions

- Created Voyageur, a game of procedural exploration
- Developed the Improv grammar library (world model-aware generation)
- Designed the filter stack architecture balancing uniqueness and coherence
