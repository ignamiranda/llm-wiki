# George Buckenham

## Bio

George Buckenham is a creator of Twitterbots and durational generative art who analyzed Twitterbots as procedural storytelling platforms. He identified key techniques including probability space curation, worldbuilding through fragmentation, and broken bots as aesthetic objects. He authored Chapter 27 "Things You Can Do with Twitterbots."

## Key Contributions

- Created Twitterbots and durational generative art
- Analyzed Twitterbots as procedural storytelling platforms
- Identified key techniques: probability space curation, fragmentation worldbuilding, broken bots as aesthetic objects
- Authored Chapter 27 "Things You Can Do with Twitterbots"
