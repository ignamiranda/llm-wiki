# Jakub Stokalski

## Bio

Jakub Stokalski is a game director at 11 Bit Studios (Warsaw, Poland). He co-authored Chapter 19 ("Beyond Fun in Frostpunk") in the 2019 edited collection *Procedural Storytelling in Game Design*, examining how Frostpunk uses systems-driven narrative to explore themes beyond traditional game entertainment.

## Key Contributions

- Game direction on Frostpunk (11 Bit Studios)
- Procedural storytelling design focusing on thematic depth and player-driven narrative
