# Dominik Borawski

## Bio

Dominik Borawski is a researcher and co-author of the 2026 paper "From World-Gen to Quest-Line: A Dependency-Driven Prompt Pipeline for Coherent RPG Generation" (arXiv:2604.25482). His work focuses on LLM-based procedural content generation for RPGs.

## Key Contributions

- Co-author of the [[dependency-driven-prompt-pipeline]] approach for multi-stage RPG generation (2026)
- Contributed to the design of structured intermediate representations for LLM-based narrative generation
