# Zach Adams

## Bio

Zach Adams, also known as Threetoe, is Tarn Adams' brother and the co-designer/story writer for Dwarf Fortress. He writes narratives and stories that serve as creative inspiration for the game's engine features. Tarn Adams then attempts to build game mechanics capable of generating similar emergent narratives within Dwarf Fortress's simulation.

## Key Contributions

- Co-designer of Dwarf Fortress, providing narrative direction and creative vision
- Writes stories that drive engine feature development — Tarn builds systems to enable the kinds of emergent narratives Zach creates
- The "Threetoe" stories are a well-known part of the Dwarf Fortress community
