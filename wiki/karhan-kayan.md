# Karhan Kayan

## Bio

Karhan Kayan is a co-author of Infinigen-Articulated (2025), contributing to the development of procedurally generated articulated assets for robotics simulation. The work extends the [[infinigen-procedural-scenes]] ecosystem into the robotics domain with 18 categories of jointed objects.
