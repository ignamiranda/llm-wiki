# Mapgen3 — Abandoned Experiment

## Summary
Mapgen3 was Amit Patel's failed attempt to port Mapgen2 from Flash to HTML5 while simultaneously adopting new algorithms and data structures. The project was abandoned after producing bugs including parallel rivers that connect incorrectly, rivers passing through lakes, and complete render failures. The failure taught a valuable lesson about managing change scope.

## Lessons Learned
The root cause was changing three things at once:
1. Language (Flash/ActionScript → HTML5/JavaScript)
2. Algorithms (new map generation approaches)
3. Data structures (new representation)

The successful approach was incremental:
- 2017: Studied new data structures in isolation
- 2017: Experimented with new algorithms in separate small projects
- 2017: Ported Mapgen2 to HTML5 + new data structures (result: Mapgen2 HTML5)
- 2018: Integrated everything into Mapgen4
