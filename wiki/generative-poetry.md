# Generative Poetry

## Definition

Generative poetry is the algorithmic production of poetic text through computational means. Approaches range from grammar-based systems (Tracery, Improv) and Markov chain models to neural network text generation. In procedural storytelling, generative poetry creates site-specific or context-responsive verse, as in the [[computational-flaneur]] where GPS-triggered poetry responds to the player's physical location.

## Key Properties

- Grammar-based: recursive symbol expansion with rhythmic constraints
- Statistical: Markov chains or neural nets trained on poetic corpora
- Site-specific: generation triggered by location, context, or game state
- Variable reward: unpredictability of quality creates emergent meaning when generated text matches environment
