# Jason Grinblat

## Bio

Jason Grinblat is the co-founder of Freehold Games and co-creator of Caves of Qud, a deeply procedural science-fantasy roguelike. He developed the technique of "historical rationalization" for generating subjective, biased world histories where effect precedes cause — creating procedurally generated lore that feels authentic and embedded. His work explores apophenia (player pattern-overperception) as a deliberate narrative design tool. He authored Chapter 15 "Generating Histories" in Procedural Storytelling in Game Design.

## Key Contributions

- Developed "historical rationalization" for procedural history generation
- Pioneered effect-precedes-cause world-building in procedural generation
- Explored apophenia as a narrative design tool
- Co-developed Caves of Qud's chunky procgen architecture composing multiple techniques
- Presented at GDC and Roguelike Celebration on procedural history and generation systems
