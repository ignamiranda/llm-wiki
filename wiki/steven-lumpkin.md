# Steven Lumpkin

## Bio

Steven Lumpkin is a game designer at Guerrilla Games. He authored Chapter 20 ("Procedural Storytelling in Dungeons & Dragons") in the 2019 edited collection *Procedural Storytelling in Game Design*, analyzing tabletop RPG frameworks — particularly Dungeon World's Moves, Dangers, and failing-forward mechanics — as procedural content generation systems driven by human pattern recognition. He is also known for his work on the West Marches campaign playstyle.

## Key Contributions

- Chapter author on procedural storytelling in D&D and TTRPGs
- West Marches campaign framework design
- Game design at Guerrilla Games
