# Gregory Knapp

## Bio / 简介

Gregory Knapp is a researcher affiliated with Brigham Young University's DRAGN Lab (Data Retrieval and Analysis Group for NLP). His research focuses on procedural quest generation for role-playing games, combining knowledge graph representations with fine-tuned language models. He is the first author of the CHI 2023 paper "Personalized Quest and Dialogue Generation in Role-Playing Games: A Knowledge Graph- and Language Model-based Approach."

## Key Contributions / 主要贡献

- First author of the CHI 2023 paper on KG+LM hybrid quest generation
- Developed the DRAGN Town quest generation system integrating Neo4j knowledge graphs with fine-tuned GPT-2
- Advanced the field of personalized procedural quest generation
