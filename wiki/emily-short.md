# Emily Short

## Bio

Emily Short is a leading figure in interactive fiction and procedural narrative. She created the Character Engine (Spirit AI), worked on Versu (a rich social simulation system), and developed Tarot of the Parrigues (a tarot-based procedural storytelling game). Her framework for authoring tools (Chapter 28) covers the first five minutes principle, sculptural writing, variety traps, and the founder principle for designing tools that empower rather than constrain authors. She has written extensively on interactive narrative design, including the "Bowls of Oatmeal" blog post that influenced the field's vocabulary.

## Key Contributions

- Created the Character Engine at Spirit AI for procedural character dialogue
- Developed Tarot of the Parrigues, a tarot-based procedural storytelling game
- Articulated the authoring tools framework including first five minutes, sculptural writing, variety traps, and founder principle
- Worked on Versu, a rich social simulation interactive fiction platform
