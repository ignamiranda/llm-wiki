# Damon Daylamani-Zad

## Bio

Damon Daylamani-Zad is a co-author of "Intelligent Generation of Graphical Game Assets: A Conceptual Framework and Systematic Review of the State of the Art" (arXiv:2311.10129, 2023). The paper provides a systematic review of 200 papers on procedural generation of graphical game assets and proposes a conceptual framework for PCG asset generation.

## Key Contributions

- **Systematic review of graphical game asset PCG** (2023): Co-author of the 200-paper survey
