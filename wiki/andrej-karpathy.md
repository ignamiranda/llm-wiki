# Andrej Karpathy

## Bio

Andrej Karpathy is a computer scientist specializing in deep learning and artificial intelligence. He was a founding member and later research scientist at OpenAI, served as Senior Director of AI at Tesla (leading the computer vision team for Autopilot), and briefly returned to OpenAI. He is known for creating the LLM Wiki pattern — a method for AI agents to build and maintain persistent knowledge bases. He also created influential educational projects including CS231n (Stanford's convolutional neural networks course), nanoGPT, and microGPT, and is a proponent of open-source AI development through projects like the "open-ground" concept for collaborative research.

## Key Contributions

- **LLM Wiki Pattern** — Created the "knowledge is compiled once and kept current" approach to AI-assisted knowledge management
- **OpenAI Co-founder** — Founding member and key researcher at OpenAI
- **Tesla Autopilot** — Led computer vision and AI for Tesla's self-driving program (2017-2022)
- **CS231n** — Stanford's seminal course on Convolutional Neural Networks for Visual Recognition
- **nanoGPT / microGPT** — Minimalist educational implementations of GPT transformers
- **Auto Research** — System for closed-loop AI-driven research experimentation
- **Open Ground** — Concept for untrusted distributed research collaboration
