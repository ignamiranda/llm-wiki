# Behind the Design of Eye of the Temple — Out on Quest 2

## Summary

Eye of the Temple was released on Quest 2 in a co-development partnership with Salmi Games. The article links to a series of design deep-dives covering inspirations, the philosophy of embodied immersion, and the engineering behind dynamic room-scale gameplay without teleportation.

## Content

The Quest 2 release represented a major milestone for Eye of the Temple, bringing the room-scale VR adventure to the most popular VR platform. Co-developed with Salmi Games, the port adapted the original PC VR version's mechanics to the standalone headset while preserving the core experience of physically walking through a large Indiana Jones-style temple.

The release was accompanied by a series of design articles exploring the game's inspirations, how embodied immersion works in practice, and the technical challenges of enabling players to traverse large virtual spaces within a single room-scale play area. The game maintained a strong critical reception with a 4.7/5 user rating, validating the design approach of physical movement over teleportation.
