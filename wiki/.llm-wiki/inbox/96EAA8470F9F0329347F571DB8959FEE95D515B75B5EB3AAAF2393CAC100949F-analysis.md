## Source Summary
Brian Butler presents a GDC talk on how Caves of Qud uses Wave Function Collapse (WFC) for tile-based map generation. Unlike the common tile-mode approach, Caves of Qud uses WFC's texture mode — starting from hand-authored map samples and generating new maps that preserve their character. Shows examples of palaces, underground tunnels, circular towers, and villages all generated from different WFC samples.
