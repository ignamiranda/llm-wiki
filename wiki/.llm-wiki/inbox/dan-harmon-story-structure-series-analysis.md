## Source Summary
Six-part tutorial series by Dan Harmon on story structure, originally published on the Channel 101 wiki. The series presents a circular story model (the "Story Circle" or "Harmon Circle") — an 8-step narrative pattern: a character in a zone of comfort wants something, enters an unfamiliar situation, adapts, gets what they wanted, pays a price, returns, and has changed. The series grounds this model in biology/psychology/sociology (102), simplifies it to caveman terms (103), maps it to Joseph Campbell's monomyth (104), adapts it for television (105), and applies it to 5-minute pilot writing (106).
