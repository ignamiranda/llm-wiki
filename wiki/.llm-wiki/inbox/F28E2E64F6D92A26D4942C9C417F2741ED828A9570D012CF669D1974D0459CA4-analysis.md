## Source Summary / 来源摘要
"Procedural Storytelling in Game Design" (CRC Press, 2019) is an edited collection edited by Tanya X. Short and Tarn Adams (with Chris Avellone), featuring 28 chapters by game industry practitioners across 5 sections. The book examines how traditional storytelling tools (characterization, world-building, theme, momentum, atmosphere) can be adapted for procedurally generated game narratives, with specific case studies from Spelunky, The Sims, Dwarf Fortress, Frostpunk, Caves of Qud, Duskers, We Happy Few, and other notable games.
