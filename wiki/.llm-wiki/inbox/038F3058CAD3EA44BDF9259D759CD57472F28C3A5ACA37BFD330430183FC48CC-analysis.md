A video by Nate (creator of Open Brain) comparing Andrej Karpathy's LLM Wiki pattern (compile-time synthesis, file-based, AI-as-writer) with Open Brain (query-time synthesis, structured database, AI-as-reader). Proposes a hybrid model where Open Brain serves as the authoritative structured data store and a wiki layer is generated on demand as a compiled view.
