## Source Summary / 来源摘要
A technical report by Jonathan Doran and Ian Parberry (LARC-2011-02, University of North Texas) analyzing ~3000 quests from four MMORPGs (Eve Online, World of Warcraft, Everquest, Vanguard: Saga of Heroes). The analysis reveals a common underlying structure across all quests, expressible as a BNF grammar with 9 NPC motivations, 23+ strategies, and 20 atomic actions. The authors describe a prototype procedural quest generator written in Prolog with a Java applet front-end.
