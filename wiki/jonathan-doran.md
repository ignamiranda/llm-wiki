# Jonathan Doran

## Bio / 简介

Jonathan (Jonathon) Doran was a researcher in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at the University of North Texas. His primary research contribution is the structural analysis of RPG quests and the development of a prototype procedural quest generator based on that analysis, co-authored with Ian Parberry.

## Key Contributions / 主要贡献

- Co-author of "A Prototype Quest Generator Based on a Structural Analysis of Quests from Four MMORPGs" (2011) with Ian Parberry
- Co-author of the precursor paper "Towards Procedural Quest Generation: A Structural Analysis of RPG Quests" (LARC-2010-02)
- Contributions to the structural decomposition of quests into NPC motivations, strategies, and atomic actions
- Implemented the Prolog-based quest generator with Java front-end
