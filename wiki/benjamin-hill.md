# Benjamin Hill

## Bio

Benjamin Hill is the co-creator of Duskers and collaborated with Tim Keenan on developing techniques for integrating curated narrative with procedural gameplay. He co-authored Chapter 8 "Curated Narrative in Duskers."

## Key Contributions

- Co-created Duskers, a game about drone exploration of derelict spaceships
- Co-developed the curated narrative approach for procedural games
- Co-authored Chapter 8 "Curated Narrative in Duskers"
