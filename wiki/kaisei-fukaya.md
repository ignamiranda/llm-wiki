# Kaisei Fukaya

## Bio

Kaisei Fukaya is the first author of "Intelligent Generation of Graphical Game Assets: A Conceptual Framework and Systematic Review of the State of the Art" (arXiv:2311.10129, 2023). The paper systematically reviews 200 papers on procedural generation of visual game assets including clouds, buildings, vegetation, and terrains.

## Key Contributions

- **Systematic review of graphical game asset PCG** (2023): 200-paper survey proposing a conceptual framework
