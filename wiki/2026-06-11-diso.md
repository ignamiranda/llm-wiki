# DISO — Differentiable Iso-Surface Extraction Package

## Summary

DISO (Differentiable Iso-Surface Extraction) is a Python/CUDA library providing fast differentiable Marching Cubes (DiffMC) and Dual Marching Cubes (DiffDMC) implementations, enabling gradient backpropagation through mesh extraction for neural 3D reconstruction pipelines.

## Content

The library implements two algorithms: DiffMC (standard marching cubes) and DiffDMC (dual marching cubes) with full CUDA acceleration and PyTorch integration. Supporting optional learnable grid deformation, batch training, and checkpointing, it achieves 2-6x speedup over DMTet and FlexiCubes with lower VRAM consumption. Outputs watertight manifold meshes with optional quad mesh support.

## Key Takeaways

- Two algorithms: DiffMC and DiffDMC
- Significantly faster and more memory-efficient than DMTet/FlexiCubes
- Supports batch training and gradient-based deformation
- pip-installable: `pip install diso`
- 327 stars, used in Neumanifold (WACV 2025)
