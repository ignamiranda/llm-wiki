A Rust library for 2D & 3D procedural generation with Model synthesis/Wave function Collapse, also available for the Bevy engine. Uses AC-4 constraint solver. Focuses on grid-based use-cases such as terrain or structures generation. Goals: Fast, Output format agnostic, Ergonomic, Well documented. Features: Model variations with rotation, Socket-based adjacency constraints, Grid loop support, Observers, Generator-human interaction. Dual-licensed MIT/Apache-2.0. 154 stars, 12 forks. Rust 100%.
