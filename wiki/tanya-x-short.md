# Tanya X. Short

## Bio

Tanya X. Short is a game designer and co-founder of Kitfox Games (known for Moon Hunters, Boyfriend Dungeon, and The Shrouded Isle). She has been a prominent voice in procedural generation and narrative game design. She co-edited (with Tarn Adams) the 2019 collection "Procedural Storytelling in Game Design," which brought together practitioners from across the games industry to document the state of the art in procedural narrative.

## Key Contributions

- Co-editor of "Procedural Storytelling in Game Design" (CRC Press, 2019)
- Game design work on Moon Hunters (cooperative action RPG with procedural personality systems)
- Founder of Kitfox Games, an independent game studio
- Advocate for thoughtful procedural generation that serves narrative goals
