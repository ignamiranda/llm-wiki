# April Update: Fire, Blades, Speedrun Mode

## Summary

A content update for Eye of the Temple adding fire hazards, swinging blade obstacles, a speedrun mode with accelerated platforms, and a 3rd person spectator camera for streaming.

## Content

The April 2017 update brought new hazards and a replay-focused mode to Eye of the Temple. Fire hazards and swinging blades were added as environmental obstacles, increasing the variety of challenges players must navigate in physical space.

A speedrun mode was introduced featuring accelerated platform movement, designed for players who wanted to test their mastery of the level flow. A 3rd person spectator camera was also added, making it easier to stream or record gameplay. The post also noted that a restricted field of view near large falls helped reduce motion sickness, an important consideration in VR design.

## Key Takeaways

- Speedrun mode with accelerated platform movement
- 3rd person spectator camera for streaming
- Field of view restriction reduces motion sickness near falls
