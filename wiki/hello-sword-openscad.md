# Hello-Sword (OpenSCAD)

## Definition

A minimal, single-file OpenSCAD implementation by mugenZebra that procedurally generates sword blade geometry using constructive solid geometry (CSG) operations. Written entirely in OpenSCAD, it is the simplest and most lightweight approach to procedural sword generation in this survey.

## Key Properties

- **Language**: OpenSCAD (100%)
- **Commits**: 1 (single-file project)
- **Output**: Parametric 3D CAD model
- **Use case**: 3D printing, prototyping, CAD visualisation
- **Approach**: Constructive solid geometry operations in OpenSCAD scripting language

## Examples

- Blade cross-section defined by parametric curves
- Suitable for export to STL for 3D printing
- Preview renders of blade geometry from front and rotated angles
