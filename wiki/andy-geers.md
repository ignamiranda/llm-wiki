# Andy Geers

## Bio

Andy Geers is an independent iOS developer who created the Dual Marching Cuboids algorithm for converting depth-map data into watertight 3D meshes suitable for 3D printing. The algorithm powers his Little Buildings app, which lets users construct 3D printable buildings from 2D depth maps.

## Key Contributions

- Invented the Dual Marching Cuboids algorithm
- Created the Little Buildings iOS app
- Published detailed explanation of greedy meshing, dual contouring, and surface tracking
