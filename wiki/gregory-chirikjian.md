# Gregory S. Chirikjian

## Bio

Gregory S. Chirikjian is a professor at the National University of Singapore (Department of Mechanical Engineering) and formerly at Johns Hopkins University. His research spans robotics, computational geometry, mechanics, and shape abstraction. He supervised the Marching-Primitives project and leads the Chirikjian Lab.

## Key Contributions

- Supervised Marching-Primitives (CVPR 2023 Highlight)
- Extensive research in robotics, kinematics, and geometry
- Author of numerous books and papers on robotic manipulation
