# First Peek at Levels in The Cluster

## Summary

Early development progress on The Cluster, a 2.5D platformer featuring non-linear exploration within a continuous procedurally generated world, built in Unity.

## Content

The Cluster was an early Unity project exploring non-linear 2.5D platforming in a continuous procedurally generated world. The post shared some of the first screenshots of the evolving level designs, showing how procedural generation could create an interconnected space for player-driven exploration.

Rather than discrete hand-crafted levels, The Cluster aimed for a seamless world where terrain and obstacles were generated as the player moved through it. This approach required solving challenges around coherence, difficulty balancing, and ensuring the generated spaces remained fun to traverse.

## Key Takeaways

- Early Unity development of The Cluster
- 2.5D non-linear procedural platformer with continuous world generation
- Emphasis on exploration-driven gameplay rather than discrete levels
