# Dan Harmon

## Bio
Dan Harmon (born January 3, 1973) is an American screenwriter, producer, and showrunner. He created the NBC sitcom Community and co-created the Adult Swim animated series Rick and Morty with Justin Roiland. He co-founded [[channel-101]] with Rob Schrab as a monthly short film festival. His influential Story Structure series, published on the Channel 101 wiki, introduced the [[harmon-story-circle]] — an 8-step circular narrative model that maps [[joseph-campbell]]'s [[monomyth]] to practical screenwriting.

## Key Contributions
- Creator of the [[harmon-story-circle]] — an 8-step circular narrative model
- Creator of Community (NBC, 2009–2014, Yahoo Screen 2015)
- Co-creator of Rick and Morty (Adult Swim, 2013–present)
- Co-founder of [[channel-101]] short film festival
- Author of the Story Structure series (101–106) on the Channel 101 wiki
