# Adam Saltsman

## Bio

Adam Saltsman is a game designer at Finji, known for creating Canabalt (the influential infinite runner) and Overland (the procedural road trip strategy game). He developed the plot vs. story distinction — games generate plots through systems and mechanics, but players generate stories through interpretation and social sharing — and introduced the concept of "froth" (post-game storytelling) from LARP culture into game design discourse. He authored Chapter 23 "Plot Generators" in Procedural Storytelling in Game Design.

## Key Contributions

- Developed the plot vs. story distinction for procedural narrative design
- Introduced the concept of "froth" from LARP culture to game design
- Created influential games Canabalt and Overland demonstrating procedural narrative principles
