# Google AI Challenge Update

## Summary

The ant AI bot reached rank 22 worldwide, first place in Denmark, and 3rd in C# category in the Google AI Challenge. The improvement process relied heavily on automated tournament testing.

## Content

Rune's bot in the Google AI Challenge (ants) climbed to rank 22 worldwide, securing first place in Denmark and 3rd place in the C# category. The competition involved programming an AI to control a colony of ants, competing against dozens of opponents per match.

The key to rapid improvement was an automated iterative development loop. The bot would play hundreds of games overnight against previous versions, serving as an A/B testing framework to evaluate each change. Small tactical refinements — such as understanding the majority-based battle resolution system where the larger army wins — produced significant ranking gains.

## Key Takeaways

- Rank 22 worldwide in Google AI Challenge
- First in Denmark, 3rd in C#
- Automated A/B tournament testing for iterative improvement
