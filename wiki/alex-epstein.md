# Alex Epstein

## Bio

Alex Epstein is a narrative designer at Compulsion Games who developed the concept of "dirty narrative" — intentionally fragmenting story information and requiring player interpretation through gaps, ambiguities, and inconsistencies. His work on We Happy Few demonstrated five specific techniques for dirty procedural narrative, including unreliable sources, contradictory accounts, and incomplete documentation that players must actively piece together. He authored Chapter 18 "Dirty Procedural Narrative in We Happy Few" in Procedural Storytelling in Game Design.

## Key Contributions

- Developed the "dirty narrative" concept for procedural storytelling
- Identified five techniques for dirty narrative in We Happy Few
- Pioneered intentional ambiguity and fragmentation as narrative design tools
