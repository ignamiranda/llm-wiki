# Felix Westin (Fewes)

## Bio

Felix Westin, known online as Fewes, is a developer who posted a refined version of the erosion filter technique as a Shadertoy in 2023. His version built on [[clay-john|Clay John]]'s original *Eroded Terrain Noise* and presented the technique with vastly more polished visuals including snow-capped mountains, green valleys, and blue water.

## Key Contributions

- Refined *Terrain Erosion Noise* Shadertoy (2023) — visual polish and slight tweaks to the erosion effect
- Demonstrated the technique's potential for high-quality terrain rendering
