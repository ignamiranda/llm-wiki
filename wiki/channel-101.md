# Channel 101

## Definition
Channel 101 is a monthly short film festival and online community co-founded by [[dan-harmon]] and Rob Schrab. Participants submit 5-minute pilot episodes, which are screened at live events and voted on by the audience. Successful shows continue monthly; unsuccessful ones are cancelled. The format's strict time constraint (5 minutes / 300 seconds) made it an ideal laboratory for developing the [[harmon-story-circle]].

## Key Properties
- Monthly screening format with audience voting
- 5-minute time limit for pilot episodes
- Shows that pass screening continue as monthly series; failed shows are "cancelled"
- Harmon's Story Structure series was written for the Channel 101 wiki as guidance for potential participants
