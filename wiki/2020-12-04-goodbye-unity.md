# Goodbye Unity

## Summary

After 12 years at Unity Technologies — watching the company grow from roughly 20 employees to over 3000 — the decision was made to leave and pursue full-time indie development. This freed focus to ship Eye of the Temple and begin work on The Big Forest.

## Content

The departure from Unity marked a major career transition. Over 12 years the company had transformed from a small Copenhagen startup into a global corporation. Leaving meant stepping away from stable employment to bet on the success of independently developed games.

The immediate priorities after leaving were clear: finish and release Eye of the Temple, the VR game that had been in development, and then begin prototyping The Big Forest as the next major project. This shift to full-time indie development removed the constraint of building games only in spare time.

- Left to pursue full-time indie development
