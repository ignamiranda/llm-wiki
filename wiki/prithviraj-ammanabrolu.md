# Prithviraj Ammanabrolu

## Bio

Prithviraj Ammanabrolu is a researcher in artificial intelligence specializing in narrative intelligence, interactive fiction, and natural language processing. His work bridges AI and game design, particularly in the area of procedurally generating narrative content for text-adventure games. He was a PhD student at the Georgia Institute of Technology, affiliated with the Entertainment Intelligence Lab directed by Mark Riedl.

## Key Contributions

- First author on "Toward Automated Quest Generation in Text-Adventure Games" (2019), presenting Markov and neural approaches to quest generation
- Research on narrative understanding and generation in interactive fiction environments
- Work on reinforcement learning for text-adventure games
