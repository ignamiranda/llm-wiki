# Chris Avellone

## Bio

Chris Avellone is an award-winning game writer and narrative designer who has worked on some of the most celebrated narrative-driven RPGs in the industry, including Planescape: Torment, Fallout: New Vegas, Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II, and Divinity: Original Sin II. He contributed to the 2019 collection "Procedural Storytelling in Game Design," bringing his perspective as a primarily authored-narrative writer engaging with procedural approaches.

## Key Contributions

- Writer for Planescape: Torment — widely regarded as one of the greatest game narratives
- Lead narrative designer for Fallout: New Vegas
- Contributor to "Procedural Storytelling in Game Design" (CRC Press, 2019)
- Bridge between traditional authored game writing and procedural narrative techniques
